Georgia

"Wisdom, Justice, Moderation"

Georgia, located in the southeastern United States, has a rich and diverse history. It was originally inhabited by Native American tribes, including the Cherokee, Creek, and Seminole. In 1733, the British colony of Georgia was founded by James Oglethorpe as a place for debtors and the poor to start anew. It was the last of the original Thirteen Colonies to be established.
