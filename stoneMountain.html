Stone Mountain

"Atlanta's favorite destination for family fun"

One of the most notable features of Stone Mountain is the Confederate Memorial Carving, the largest bas-relief sculpture in the world. Carved into the side of the mountain, this impressive sculpture depicts three Confederate leaders: Jefferson Davis, Robert E. Lee, and Stonewall Jackson.
