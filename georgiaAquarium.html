Georgia Aquarium

"Dive into the fun"

The Georgia Aquarium, located in Atlanta, is one of the world's largest aquariums. Encounter diverse marine life, including whale sharks and beluga whales. Explore unique exhibits and habitats, learn about marine conservation, and enjoy daily shows and feedings. Whether you're a marine enthusiast or looking for a family-friendly adventure, the Georgia Aquarium offers an unforgettable experience.
